ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Innovative Agricultural Technology Solutions

Our first goal is to become a friend of the farmer with our technology and services! As a company, we regularly fly multispectral and lidar sensor drones in crop fields and forest areas and collect the data obtained in our data center. With our specially prepared Machine learning algorithms on the acquired database, we provide comprehensive insights for better agricultural decisions.

✓

Identify areas where plant development is below normal or underdeveloped

✓

Detect soil surface anomalies and moisture levels in real-time

✓

Receive analytical reports with instant notifications through mobile and web platforms

✓

Reduce operational costs - no need for excessive manpower, energy, or equipment

✓

Make data-driven decisions with our expert recommendations
