Datagron: Digital Livestock

Datagron is a professional platform that manages livestock farms using scientific data. We optimize rations in seconds based on animal breeds and target productivity, reducing feed waste and ensuring maximum efficiency of farm resources.

Scientific Nutrition Database

End guesswork in feeding. Datagron provides precise energy and nutrient values (protein, fiber, etc.) for various feed types. Use this intellectual database to scientifically boost milk and meat production while maintaining full control over your financial costs.

Smart Monitoring

Our system analyzes real-time temperature, humidity, and pressure for your specific location to help protect animal welfare. Choose from extensive livestock categories—including cattle, poultry, and small ruminants—to take the most accurate management steps for each breed based on your local environment.

Strategic Resource Planning

No more estimating annual feed needs and land resources. Enter your animal count and productivity targets; Datagron instantly calculates the required feed tonnage and the exact hectares needed to grow it. We empower farmers to plan resources with 100% precision.
